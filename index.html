2014-2018 Earthquakes

Between 2014 and 2018, inclusively, the US Geological Survey has recorded 692 earthquakes of magnitude at least 6. Their locations are represented by points on the globe.

You can rotate the view by dragging the globe and zoom using your mouse wheel. Hovering your mouse over a point provides more information.
